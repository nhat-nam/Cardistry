Welcome to the World of Cardistry!

Cardistry is something you see, feel, and love. The world of cardistry is broad with endless techniques, sleights, and mechanics. It's a tool used to impress and to inspire.

The Starting Point

Before anyone can start, they need a proper deck of cards. The kinds that professionals use. There are a range of amazing companies like Theory 11, Ellusionist, Bicycle, that produce amazing playing cards. Find the right deck for you!

Getting familiar

The most important part of a deck of cards are, of course, the cards. You want to get the feel of the cards and leave control over your cards. Establish the fact that this is now your deck of cards that and you will master cardistry with it.
